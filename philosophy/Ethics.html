Ethics

Ethics is the study of moral definitions and actions. Ethical theory explores the moral implications of actions. Ethics has a variety of sub-catagories that define its scope of study. this can range from bioethics, deontology, utilitarianism and virtue ethics.

Consequenstialism

This is the branch of Ethics that is primarily concerned with actions. The definition of what is morally "right" and "wrong" is defined by what effect the action has after it is commited.

Deontology

Deontolgy is the study of ethics that is concered if wether a action is right or wrong based on a series of rules. In this case, the action itself, and the context prior to the action is more important than the consequences.
