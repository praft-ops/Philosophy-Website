David Hume

Hume was a primary proponent of emotivisim. Generally, he asserted reason alone cannot be a motive to the will. Moral distinctions are not derived from resason. Moral distinctions are derived from moral sentiments. Essentially, this is trademark of Hume which is Ethical Anti-Rationalism.
