Descartes

Descarte is the prime example of continental philosophy. Famous for the line of "Ergo, Cogito, Sum". In english, "I think therefore I am.". This statement generally is a proponent of mind-body dualism. This exemplified and defined the mind-body problem. This highlights a metaphyisical dualism between the mind which is an essence of thinking and matter.
