Kant

Kant is the primary figure in modern philosophy. He brought together early rationalism and empiricisim. Much of the terms used today in philosophy come from Kants three critiques. The fundemental idea that is central to all of Kant's work is human autonomy. This results in a teleological worldview that Kant describes in borth theoretical and practical parts of his philosophical work.
