Epistemology

Epistemology is the study of knowledge. This branch of philosophy studies what can be known or not known and studies the boundries of knowledge. This branch of philosophy extends heavily into all disiplines of philosophy as the nature of knowing comprises all boundries of thought. Prior to any philosophical theory is proposed, the epistemological considerations are defined to lay the structure of logical argument.

Foundationalism

Foundationalism is the branch of epistemology that is primarly concerned with self-evident truths. Supporters of foundationalism believe that self evident truths provide the structure for all things to be known. Descates is a famous foundationalist for "Ergo Cogito Sum".
